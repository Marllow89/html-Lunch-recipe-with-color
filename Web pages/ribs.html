Main Course - Honey glazed ribs

These honey-glazed ribs are easy to make. Delicious served either hot or at room temperature. The sauce is great served over rice.

Prep time for this meal is around 15 minutes. Cook time is around 60 minutes.

Ingredients for this recipe:

  • 2 kilograms pork spareribs
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions for this recipe:


  1. Preparing the oven

    2. Preheat the oven to 190 degrees C.

  2. Preparing the meat

    3. Slice ribs into individual pieces. Combine honey, say sauce, vinegar, brown sugar and garlic in a large bowl. Stir until honey and sugar are completely dissolved, then stir in baking soda. The mixture will begin to foam. Transfer ribs to the bowl ant turn the coat.

  3. Arranging meat

    4. Cover a cookie sheet with foil, arrange ribs meat-side on the sheet. Pour excess sauce over ribs and sprinkle with garlic salt.

  4. Baking ribs

    5. Bake in the preheated oven for 60 minutes, turning ribs every 15 minutes.
