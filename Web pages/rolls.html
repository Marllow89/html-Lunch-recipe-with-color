Dessert - Cinnamon rolls

This is an easy cinnamon rolls recipe made from scratch with no yeast, proofing or kneading necessary. Just the thing for a quick treat.

Prep time for this meal is around 20 minutes. Cook time is around 20.

Ingredients for this recipe:

  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, divided
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, plus more as needed
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 2/3 cups whole milk
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 100 grams cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions for this recipe:


  1. Preparing the oven and baking dish

    2. Gather ingredients. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees C. Brush your baking dish with 1 tablespoon of melted butter.

  2. Making the dough

    3. Whisk flour, white sugar, baking powder and salt together in a large bowl. Work 3 tablespoons softened butter into flour mixture using your hands until butter is lentil-sized. Pour milk into flour-butter mixture and stir with a rubber spatula until a soft dough forms.

  3. Preparing the dough

    4. Turn dough onto a well-floured work surface and roll dough into a square. Brush surface of dough with remaining 2 tablespoons melted butter.

  4. Making the filling

    5. Whisk 1/4 cup white sugar, brown sugar and cinnamon together in a small bowl. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon cinnamon-sugar mixture in the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle remaining cinnamon sugar over butter-brushed dough.

  5. Making the rolls

    6. Roll the dough around filling to form a log. Cut the log into several pieces around 4-5 cm wide. Place rolls, cut side up into prepared baking dish.

  6. Baking the rolls

    7. Bake the rolls in the preheated oven until rolls are puffed and lightly golden, 20 to 25 minutes.

  7. Making the frosting

    8. Beat confectioners' sugar, cream cheese, 1/4 cup softened butter and vanilla extract together in a bowl until frosting is smooth. Top hot cinnamon rolls with frosting and serve it.
